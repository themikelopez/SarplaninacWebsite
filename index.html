A website all about one of the most effective and loyal livestock guardian dogs: the Sarplaninac.

About

I'm Mike and I am very passionate about these spectacular dogs. Being so uncommon in the United States, this website sets out to be a source of helpful information, a list of resources for current and to-be Sarplaninac owners, and a place where the community of Sarplaninac lovers can join together.

I am an aspiring front-end engineer and this is a personal project of mine that I will grow and continue to develop my skills through. I want to take this chance to say thank you to everyone who helped me with information and photography for this project.

Also, thank you for visiting my site, and if you would like to see more of my work, you can do so here.
