About

Sarplaninacs.com

This website sets out to be a source of helpful information for those that want to know more about the Sarplaninac, an amazing livestock guardian breed from the mountainous regions of former Yugoslavia.

Sarplaninacs.com is a place for the Sarplaninac community to get accurate information about the breed, read blog posts about Sarplaninacs, view photography of Sarplaninacs and find additional helpful information about becoming a Sarplaninac owner with recommended breeders we trust and know here in North America. Whether you are just discovering this breed, or have known about them for years, the community of Sarplaninac enthusiasts has been growing quickly!

We are continuing to build the Sarplaninac community here and on our Instagram page. Follow us on Instagram (@Sarplaninacdogs) to see more beautiful photos of these amazing dogs and join along in discussions!

About the creator of this website

Hello, I'm Mike!

I am a Sarplaninac owner, myself, and I am very passionate about these spectacular dogs! I met my first Sarplaninac almost 15 years ago, and I have never been the same since (I mean that in the best way possible). I've always been a lover of animals and nature, but the Sarplaninac struck me as something extremely unique and intelligent. I had never met another dog like the Sarplaninac, and since rescuing my first one, I have truly enjoyed being a steward of the breed.

Livi, my first Sarplaninac, was brought directly over from Serbia as a puppy. I was fortunate to know her for a few years from her original family. In 2012, when Livi was 7 years old, she officially became my dog. I had a few wonderful years with her before she passed. She was such an amazing dog and is always in my heart.

These days, my family now has 4 other Sarplaninacs working 40 acres in Colorado. They protect the property, the home, and the family. We have 2 females and 3 males, including my boy, Lucas. I have had Lucas since he was 8 weeks old and he is now 7 years old and a wonderful dog who works part-time on my family's property.

Each Sarplaninac I have ever met is unique; they all have their own personalities, their own likes and dislikes, and living with a Sarplaninac is in many ways like living with another person. I live and breathe Sarplaninacs and will forever be an advocate for their well-being and someone who wants to help educate the world about this amazing breed of working dog. They add so much joy and purpose to my life, and I am grateful to have been introduced to them so many years ago. I have met amazing people along the way, thanks to these dogs, and I am excited to continue this journey.

I am a front-end engineer and this is a personal project of mine that I will grow and continue to develop my skills through. I want to take this chance to say thank you to everyone who helped me with information and photography for this project.

Also, thank you for visiting my site, and if you would like to see more of my work, you can do so by visiting my personal portfolio here.
